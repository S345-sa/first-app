# Exemplars — The Gold Standard Shelf

The Critic (Forge, pass 2) judges every draft against the passages in this folder.
A model in front of the machine pulls it toward the masterworks' region far more
strongly than any description of them.

## Why this folder starts empty

These passages are copyrighted. **You** (the learner) place them here, from copies
you own, for your private study — they are never published or committed beyond
your own private repository. Keep each excerpt short (1–3 pages): the *anatomy*
matters, not the bulk.

reason, reason-behind-the-reason, the disagreements, the abandoned view and why.

## Anatomy notes (do this once per passage — it doubles the folder's power)

At the top of each file, before the excerpt, write 3–5 lines of YOUR analysis:
*what exactly* this passage does that ordinary textbooks don't. The Critic quotes
these anatomies back at drafts that fall short.
